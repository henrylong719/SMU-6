Application Guide

Regarding the bottom of the zoom in and zoom out Click the button of the zoom in is to zoom in the organization chart. The function of the zoom out button is that when clicking the button, the size of organization chart will be reduced.

This is normal size website above, when clicking zoom in, the organization chart will be larger:

when clicking the button zoom out, the size of organization chart will be reduced:

In organization Chart, the membership information of the company is displayed as Node of the chart. When the mouse moves over each node, the member information represented by each node is displayed.

by pressing the ... button, a window displayed the detailed information of the employee will be popped out. further detailed of the info please refer to spreadsheets.

Each department uses a different colour to display on the chart. People in the same department use the same colour to represent.
